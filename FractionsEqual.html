Fractions

What are fractions? When you break your leg, you fracture it in two parts.

one whole
one whole divided into 5 equal parts
shaded amount: 3 out of 5 equal parts
35 = shaded amount of the whole
Numerator = number of parts taken   |   Denominator = number of parts divided

When are two fractions equal?

46 = shaded amount of the whole
23 = shaded amount of the whole

These two shaded amounts are equal, so 46 = 23.

Rule: For any fraction, multiplying the numerator and the denominator by the same number keeps the fractions equal.
