Worksheet Maker

First line = worksheet title.
A label like Example: or Exercises: goes on its own line.
The sequence goes on the next line, using commas.
Use plain values for shown items and [value] for hidden answers.
Pink boxes are hidden-answer spaces. The row auto-scrolls while typing near the edge and snaps back when the last pink box in that row is filled.
